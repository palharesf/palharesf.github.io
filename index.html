SG Minesweeper

A modern take on the classic Minesweeper game, built entirely with vanilla JavaScript. Features multiple difficulty levels, custom board sizes, and a clean, intuitive interface. The game includes smart first-click logic to ensure you never hit a mine on your opening move, and implements the classic flagging system for marking suspected mines.

Technologies: JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3
